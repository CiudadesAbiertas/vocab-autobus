The urban public bus service vocabulary uses mainly the modules of the Transmodel vocabulary, it relies on other vocabularies (sosa, geo, kos and organization) in addition to creating some ad hoc classes and properties.

In particular, from Transmodel the following vocabularies have been reused:

  • Commons, tmcommons, to represent some common concepts and properties of the Transmodel modules. Journeys, https://w3id.org/transmodel/journeys#">tmjourneys

    • The other vocabularies used were:

    • For the taxonomies, thare have been used the Simple Knowledge Organization System (SKOS), specifically:
      • tmkoscommons for common taxonomies.
      • tmkosjourney for travel taxonomies.
    • The following vocabularies have been reused for the geographical representation of the data:
      • geo, for latitude and longitude properties in WGS84.
      • geo_core, for the properties of latitude and longitude in ETRS89.
      • geosparql for the geographical representation of the three entities.
      • sf (Simple Features Geometries), for the representation of the point sf:Point.
    • For some values of the buses or routes the vocabulary of sensors SOSA (Semantic Sensor Network Ontology) has been reused.
    • The vocabulary estraf has been reused for incidents.
    • For the address of the stop, the escjrvocabulary has been reused.
    • The vocabulary org has been used for transport organisations.
